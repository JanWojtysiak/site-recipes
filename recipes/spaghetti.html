Spaghetti recipe

Description

This simple spaghetti recipe combines al dente pasta with a rich, homemade tomato sauce made from garlic, onions, and ripe tomatoes. Topped with fresh basil and grated Parmesan, it’s a quick and comforting meal perfect for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 400g (14 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, and sauté until soft.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, topped with fresh basil and grated Parmesan if desired.
