Pizza recipe

Description

This easy homemade Margherita pizza features a crispy thin crust, fresh mozzarella, ripe tomatoes, and fragrant basil leaves. Baked to perfection in under 15 minutes, it’s a simple yet delicious Italian favorite you can enjoy anytime.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozarella, sliced
  • 1-2 fresh tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional: a pinch of dried oregano or chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add mozzarella slices and tomato slices on top.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle a little salt.
  6. Bake for 10–15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  7. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Slice and serve warm.
